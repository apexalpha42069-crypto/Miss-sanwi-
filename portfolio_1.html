Teaching Approach

My
Methodology

I apply communicative language teaching (CLT) — using role-play scenarios, structured dialogue drills, and contextual vocabulary building to keep beginners fully engaged from lesson one.

Each session is built around a real-world theme — ordering food, asking for directions, introducing yourself — so students immediately feel the relevance of what they're learning.

Visual flashcards, spaced repetition, short audio exercises, and group speaking tasks are woven into every class to address different learning styles and reinforce long-term retention.
